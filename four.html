Our City: Coimbatore

"Namma kovaikku vannakam". Coimbatore, also called the Manchester of South India, is a cosmopolitan city where you can't differentiate between greenery and traffic. It is home to the prestigious "Tamil Nadu Agricultural University", and lots of other premier educational institutions. As much as it concentrates on building the future, it still remembers its past through popular museums such as the Geedee Car Museum, Geedee Science Museum, Government Museum, Insect Museum (TNAU), Kasthuri Sreenivasan Art and Textile Museum and many more. That is not to say it lacks modern entertainment. Coimbatore also has 3 major malls, and countless shopping complexes, the most famous being Town Hall and Gandhipuram. Coimbatore is also home to a rich tapestry of temples, which draws in pilgrims and tourists alike. Apart from this, Coimbatore also offers finger licking foods that are to die for. Overall, it is the right mix of tradition, moderity and spirituality, that is bound to enthrall you.
