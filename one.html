The Internet

What is the Internet?

  1. Is a computer-based worldwide information network.
  2. Is an example of an Wide Area Network
  3. Comprises a large number of smaller interconnected networks.

What are the uses of the internet?

  1. Provides many services and commodities that increase accessibility and comfort.
  2. Can be used to access information and opportunities that were previously not open.
  3. Can be used to research, communicate, share data and documents, and a plethora of other applications.
